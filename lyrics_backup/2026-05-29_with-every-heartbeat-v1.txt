[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
Come a little closer to the firelight
Let the shadows dance across your face
I just wanna hold you through the midnight
And lose myself inside this quiet space

[Verse 1]
We’ve been driving down this endless dusty road
With the windows down letting the cool wind blow
But right here in the quiet when the engine dies
All I see is the heaven hiding in your eyes

Every breath you take is pulling me in
Like a slow-burning ember on my skin
I don’t want this evening to ever fade
Just want to rest in the warmth we made

[Chorus]

You’re the single promise I’m bound to keep
In this midnight glow where the shadows play
I’m yours completely stealing away
